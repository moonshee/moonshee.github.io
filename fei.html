Background:
The game takes place in Southern China. Canton Province. Hong Kong Island, Kowloon Harbour and New Territories host most of the game scenes. The characters are all Cantonese except the ghost of Alan Turing who communicates regularly with the village fortune teller who has a booth at today's Summer Solstice Festival. An ominous creature named FEI PAI TSU is said to haunt the village whenever there is a total solar eclipse and the villagers are all expecting such an eclipse which coincides with the festival. Therefore, the party atmosphere is somewhat dampened. Last time the FEI PAI TSU flew around the village, several children disappeared and livestock was also attacked and slaughtered. To further complicate matters, Chin-san's dojo is under seige from a ruthless rival dojo and the solstice is the deadline for Chin-san's dojo to capitulate or else the rival dojo has warned of complete destruction.
