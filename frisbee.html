Congratulations on your purchase of ULTIMATE FRISBEE, one of the best new ultimate frisbee games availables as of 2026. This is a ONE or TWO PLAYER game. In ONE PLAYER mode, the computer is the VISITOR. All the standard rules of ultimate apply and the field is 100 yards long, so perhaps a bit longer than a usual pitch. The matches are timed to 90 minutes plus injury time and weather delays in case of electric storms. The games are played at Nigoshi Field which is a daytime-only facility with no evening lights located in Tokyo, Japan. Ground rules: If the disk is thrown over the fence into the gulch, that is a turnover and warning. Three throws over the fence by one team will result in a yellow card issued to the head coach. Ten second limit for holding the disk, no travel allowed.

The directional keypad is mostly for checking for opponents and looking at the cheerleaders along with talking with teammates. Use the throw, block, etc, buttons mainly.
