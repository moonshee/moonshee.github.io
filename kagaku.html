    🛠 Chemical Developer Resource List:

    Note from Prof. Gillis: The main thing in chemistry is to learn the reactions and the names. You need to know what methane is. It's CH4. And you need to know that hydronium and hydroxide come together to form water. Water is an ionic solution. So, read chemistry books and learn the lingo, learn the reactions. That's step one. Try not to get bogged down in electron configs and shells, crystal structures, mol calculations for now. Instead, focus on what is a weak acid and is urea water soluble."
